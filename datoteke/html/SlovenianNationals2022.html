Register for this competition here.
There is a competitor limit of 80 competitors.
The base registration fee for this competition is €15 (Euro).
If your registration is cancelled before Saturday, September 10, 2022 at 2:00 AM GMT+2 you will be refunded 100% of your registration fee.
Registrants on the waiting list may be accepted onto the competitor list until Monday, September 12, 2022 at 2:00 AM GMT+2.
If you are a registered competitor you may change your registered events until Monday, September 12, 2022 at 2:00 AM GMT+2 by contacting the organization team.
No on the spot registrations will be accepted.


EN

Registration is considered completed after filling out the registration form and paying the registration fee via Stripe on the register page. Registration fee is 15€.

If you have registered and paid but the competitor limit has been reached, you will receive a spot on the waiting list. You will be notified via email once a spot for you available. If you do not move up from the waiting list until registration closes, you will get a full refund.

If you have any problems with paying the registration fee, or if you would like to change your registration, please write an e-mail to the wca.slovenia@gmail.com e-mail address.

SI

Registracija bo popolna, ko se boste prijavili na tekmovanje in plačali štartnino preko Stripe. Štartnina je 15€.

Če imate kakšne težave pri plačilu štartnine ali če bi želeli spremeniti svojo prijavo nam pišite na wca.slovenia@gmail.com.
